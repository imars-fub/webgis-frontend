Single-orbit digital terrain models derived from stereo image strips of the High-Resolution Stereo Camera (HRSC) of ESA's Mars Express

Product type: single-orbit DTMs (Level-4)

Grid resolution: 50 m per pixel

Object points derived from single orbits after bundle adjustment.

Note: this dataset has been compiled from the available level 4 single strip DTMs. The individual strips are not adjusted to each other and therefore seams are visible between the strips.

Data source: - currently not available for public download.

Product references:
Gwinner et al. (2010), Topography of Mars from global mapping by HRSC high-resolution digital terrain models and orthoimages: Characteristics and performance, doi:10.1016/j.epsl.2009.11.007

Instrument references:
Neukum et al. (2004), HRSC: The High Resolution Stereo Camera of Mars Express, ESA SP-1240 (2004), p. 19, ISBN: 92-9092-556-6
Jaumann et al. (2007), The high-resolution stereo camera (HRSC) experiment on Mars Express: Instrument aspects and experiment conduct from interplanetary cruise through the nominal mission, 10.1016/j.pss.2006.12.003

The DTM product has been mosaicked, hill-shaded and colour-coded by Freie Universitaet Berlin.
