Multi-orbit digital terrain models derived from multiple stereo image strips of the High-Resolution Stereo Camera (HRSC) of ESA's Mars Express

Product type: multi-orbit DTMs (Level-5)

Grid resolution: 50 m per pixel

Object points derived from single orbits after bundle-block adjustment.

Note: currently, only the MC11 quadrangle (east and west) is available.

Data source: - currently not available for public download.

Product references:
Gwinner et al. (2016), The High Resolution Stereo Camera (HRSC) of Mars Express and its approach to science analysis and mapping for Mars and its satellites, doi:10.1016/j.pss.2016.02.014

Instrument references:
Neukum et al. (2004), HRSC: The High Resolution Stereo Camera of Mars Express, ESA SP-1240 (2004), p. 19, ISBN: 92-9092-556-6
Jaumann et al. (2007), The high-resolution stereo camera (HRSC) experiment on Mars Express: Instrument aspects and experiment conduct from interplanetary cruise through the nominal mission, 10.1016/j.pss.2006.12.003

The DTM product has been hill-shaded and colour-coded by Freie Universitaet Berlin.
