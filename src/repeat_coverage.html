Global Mars repeat coverage map using ODE footprints

Product type: repeat coverage map - Colours correspond to the number of times each area has been mapped

Instrument: all NASA and ESA visible cameras with resolution finer than 100m/pixel

References:
P. Sidiropoulos and J. P. Muller (2015), “On the status of orbital high-resolution repeat imaging of Mars for the observation of dynamic surface processes”, Planetary and Space Science, vol. 117, pp. 207–222, Jul. 2015, DOI: 10.1016/j.pss.2015.06.017
